The Fourth International Workshop on Smart Data for Blockchain and Distributed Ledger (SDBD’24)

Co-located with ACM SIGKDD 2024, August 26th, 2024, Barcelona, Spain

Introduction

The recent emergence of powerful machine learning techniques and applications change disruptively the landscape of data and machine learning model supply chain in industry, that is, how data and machine learning models are produced, requested, built and applied. To echo this novel trend, this tutorial focuses on two major themes of recent advances in data science: data pricing (Part A) and data asset (Part B), each a self-contained 3-hour tutorial collectively presenting the theme of “Data Monetization and Collaboration in End-to-End Data Science Applications”.
This tutorial will be highly interdisciplinary and a fusion of academia foundations and industry practice. We do not assume any background knowledge and will make the essential ideas highly accessible to practitioners and graduate students.
