16:30 – 17:30 Panel: AI & Sustainable Digital Economy – When Academia Meets Industry
Panelists: Feida ZHU, Bangdao CHEN, Luyao ZHANG, Yue ZHAO

1. How can AI enhance the scalability, security, and efficiency of blockchain and Web3 applications, particularly in creating a sustainable digital economy? Or the other way around, how can Web3 benefit AI for the same goal? Can you provide examples of successful implementations or ongoing projects that illustrate this integration? 2. What role do blockchain and Web3 technologies play in fostering a more sustainable and ethically responsible digital economy? How can AI contribute to addressing challenges such as energy consumption, data privacy, and fair distribution of resources within these ecosystems? 3. How can collaboration between academia and industry drive innovation in the development of AI-powered blockchain and Web3 solutions? What are the key challenges and opportunities in translating academic research into real-world applications that support a sustainable digital economy? 4. What are the emerging trends at the intersection of AI, blockchain, and Web3 that will shape the future of the digital economy? How can academic institutions and industry leaders work together to equip the next generation of professionals with the necessary skills to thrive in this evolving landscape?
